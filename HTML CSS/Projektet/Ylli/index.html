About Porsche

Porsche is a German automobile manufacturer known for producing high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. The company was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Germany. Initially, it was an engineering firm that worked on various automotive projects, including the design of the Volkswagen Beetle.
