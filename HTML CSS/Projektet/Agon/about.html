Riding free in the mountains is our sport’s original expression. To find your own challenge, and ride by your own rules. Pitting yourself against nature’s features, while rising to the demands of the terrain in a way that’s improvised, never contrived. But the challenge of the mountain is what we make it. From tree-lined valley lines to steep powder fields, freeriding is skiing’s blank canvas — a vast palette of environments to chase your own goals and find your own horizon.
